Who We Are

Her Additions is an online business of authentic handcrafted jewelry from Nepal. More than 70 ethnic groups exist among 22 million Nepalese therefore the variety of jewelry designs exist. 90% of total gold/silver craftsmen in Nepal are into jewelry design and jewelry business. Now in 21st- century various technology has been developed but no modern types of equipment are used for the development of the jewelry so these pieces of jewelry are primitive, authentic and are incredible and beautiful in designs but due to poor marketing it is losing it place in the market. So, Her Additions promote those jewelries all around the world with a cause.
